After PROTEUSÂ¶

PROTEUS is just a project. Its results (Modelling platform, Generation tooling, convergence towards ROS exchanges, open access to Robotic platforms for outsiders, etc) are to exist after its end because of their use. The Robotic modelling language is not dependant of the project, the OARPs are for two of them to go on living their life, the Community portal will be maintained and updated during two years and so on and so forth. It is of the utmost importance to understand that the project is a community service in order to allow increase of exchange and to open market to innovative product shortening time between research centres and industry.
